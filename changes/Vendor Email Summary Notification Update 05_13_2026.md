**Job Offer Announcement Email Process**

* **Step 1 –** Buyer completes the job offer questionnaire  
* **Step 2 –** Buyer submits job offer to vendor network and receives free estimate  
* **Step 3 –** job offer announcement goes out via email to vendor network  
  * Copy of job offer goes to admin  unredacted  
  * Copy of job offer goes to buyers account unredacted  
* Step 4 – vendors receive notification via email of job offer redacted version. A copy of the job offer is also posted in the vendors account for access.   
* Step 5 – vendors will either accept or decline the offer. Vendors will be required to pay credits if they accept the offer to receive the unredacted data. Vendors can only see the unredacted version once they accept the offer via email or in their accounts.   
* Buyer will be notified and updated in real time of accepted offers only.
